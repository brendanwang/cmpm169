Reflection

I worked on this project by myself and took about as long as I thought it would take. Given that Iâ€™m finally becoming comfortable with the p5.js library, I feel that I can really start to create something special working off pre-existing examples.

This project had its ups and downs. Since I already had experience making 3D models from a previous project, setting up the space part was pretty easy. Using p5.js for handling 3D stuff made it simple to create a cool-looking space scene without much trouble. But the tricky part was getting the shooting stars to look right. Even though I knew how to use objects and vectors, making sure the shooting stars showed up and moved realistically in the 3D space was tougher than I thought. It took some trial and error to make them move in a straight line while still looking good. Overall, this project taught me not to underestimate the challenges, even in seemingly simple parts of a bigger project, and the importance of patience in problem-solving.
